Historic Moments

Established

11 Nov 1909

The Reformers in the North side of Kallooppara Palli 27 families got together and constructed a shed not far from the location of the present Parish using bamboo, areca wood (Kamuku) and palm leaves, placed a cross dedicated and worshiped.

1924 Permenant Church Building

10 Jun 1924

It was decided to have a permanent building for church services and the foundation stone was laid in 1919. The building was completed and consecrated in 1924 by the Metropolitan the Most Rev Titus II and the Suffragan Metropolitan and Rt. Rev Abraham Marthoma

1959 Golden Jubilee Year

12 Nov 1959

The Golden Jubilee of the Parish was organised with extensive programmes and Rt Rev Mathews Mar Athanasius led the Parish in its the celebrations in 1959.

Renovation Of Church Building

15 May 1976

The Metropolitan the Most Rev Dr Yuhanon Marthoma laid the foundation stone for the new church building on 15 February 1970. Placing rafter beam was done in 1972. The Diocesan Bishop Rt Rev Mathews Mar Athanasius blessed the occasion. The consecration service of the renovated church building was led by Saffragon Metropolitan Rt Rev Alexander Mar Theophilos along with Rt Rev Thomas Mar Athanasius on 15 May 1976. After consecration, the first Qurbana was offered by the Most Rev Yuhanon Marthoma Metropolitan.

1984 Platinum Jubilee Year

25 Nov 1984

The Parish celebrated its Platinum Jubilee on 25 November 1984. H G the Most Rev Dr Alexander Marthoma Metropolitan led the celebrations. A brief history of the Parish was published, which familiarised the young generation with the early days of our Parish. And the Metropolitan laid the foundation stone for the Parsonage, which was a Platinum Jubilee Memorial programme.

Navathy

14 Nov 1999

Navathy (Ninety Glorious Years) The Parish formed by our forefathers, who were inspired by naveekaranam, completed ninety years in 1999. The year-long navathy celebrations were inaugurated by Rt Rev Yuyakim Mar Kurilose, the Bishop of Ranni-Nilackel Diosece on 22 November 1998. A timeslab describing the important events in the history of the Parish was installed. Joining with the Maitree Bhavanadana Padhati two houses were constructed and gifted. A hall adjoining the church building and a separate kitchen were constructed and consecrated. The Most Rev Philippose Mar Chrysostom Metropolitan led the concluding function of the centenary celebrations on 14 November 1999.

Centinary

8 Nov 2009

Satabdi (Centenary) The centenary celebrations of the Parish were inaugurated by the Most Rev Philippose Mar Chrysostom Valia Metropolitan on 9 November 2008. A number of programmes were conducted under the auspices of various sub-committees.

The following schemes were decided to be initiated in connection with the centenary:

  1. Satabdi Smaraka Suvisheshvela Endowment Fund be collected.
  2. Centenary House Gifting scheme.
  3. Centenary Marriage Assistance for two persons.
  4. Hosting of a website for the Parish.

The concluding meeting of the centenary celebrations were held on 7 November 2009. Rt Rev Zacharias Mar Theophilos Saffragan Metropolitan inaugurated the meeting. H G Geevarthese Mar Osthathios, Metropolitan of Niranam Orthodox Diocese was present and H G Thomas Mar Koorilose, Archbishop of Thiruvalla Malankara Catholic Diosese blessed the function. Various political, religious, and community leaders participated. On 8 November 2008 Holy Mass was celebrated by the Saffragan Metropolitan, and it was followed by the Parish Day meeting. God Almighty blessed our Parish abundantly and enabled us to peacefully enter the New Century.
